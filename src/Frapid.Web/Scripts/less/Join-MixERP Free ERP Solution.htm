Become a Partner

MixERP channel partner programs bring a huge benefit to your company if you are looking forward to offer ERP solution. Apply for becoming a channel partner today to unlock the amazing benefits such as early access to features and growing revenue stream.

Why Should You Become a Partner?
Lead Referral
Boost your sales by getting access to leads in your partner portal.
Shared Fees
Enjoy highly competitive commission on sales, implementation, and data migration.
Bug Fixes
Report urgent bugs and issues on partner portal and get it fixed quickly.
Knowledge Transfer
Train your developers and consultants periodically to stay ahead in the competition.
Client Partner
Increase your chance of closing the deal by being assigned a client partner from us.
Marketing Kits
Get access to our latest marketing kits, presentation files, videos, and more.
Partner Requirements
Certification
You should have at least one MixERP certified developer during your enrollment.
Sales Plan
You should present a clear sales target, plan, and strategy for MixERP promotion.
Customer Support
You should be willing to provide first-line support to your MixERP clients.
Website
You should adjust your website with MixERP information in your local lanaguge.
Unlock Partner Benefits
Complete this application and we will get back to you within 2 business days.
